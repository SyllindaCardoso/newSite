About me

My name is Syllinda, but everbody calls me Syll.
I have about 30 years of experience teaching English as a second language.
I teach English to children, teenagers, and adults.
I have 10 years of experience teaching online.
I have the CELTA Certificate and a post graduation in English Language and Literature.
I am from Brazil originally, but I lived in London, England for 8 years,
and in Vancouver, Canada, for 2 years.

Material

When I choose my course material, I always take into account the material press and CEFR - Common European Framework of Reference.

They are also getting ready for the Cambridge Certificates, such as IELTS.
